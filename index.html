Explore WYK in Minecraft
We've an idea of creating a near-to-scale replica of our school, Wah Yan College, Kowloon in Minecraft - and we turned it into a reality. After months and months of work together with our friends, our beloved WYK campus now lives in Minecraft.

What is it?
Project WYK is a leisure project that began in April 2020 as we tried our best to make a near-to-scale replica of the campus. And after months and months of work and endless building, our beloved WYK campus now lives in Minecraft.
