About
Project WYK is a leisure project that began in April 2020 as we tried our best to make a near-to-scale replica of the campus. The Project WYK map contains three major parts: the campus itself, custom-made fun to play minigames, and a railway system connecting the above two areas. And after months and months of work and endless building, we are proud to present the Project WYK map. Click a tile to learn about it.
