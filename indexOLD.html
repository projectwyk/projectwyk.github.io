Explore. Be Amazed.

Project WYK began in April 2020 when the idea to build our secondary school in Minecraft was suggested. Since, a dedicated server was opened. Friends came to help us out. Construction was slow as we tried our best to make a near-to-scale replica, following numerous photos taken and research online. ​Progress was steadily made. And after months and months of work and endless building, we are proud to present our beloved WYK campus in Minecraft.
